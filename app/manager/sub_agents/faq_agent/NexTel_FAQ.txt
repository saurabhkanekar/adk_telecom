### NexTel Xstream Box & NexTel Smart TV FAQs

1. What is NexTel Xstream TV?

The NexTel Xstream TV is a new age DTH connection that enhances any television into a smart TV. It enables you to watch online content via various OTT apps such as Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Hotstar, etc. - on your big TV screen. The built-in Chromecast also enables voice search with Google Assistant.

2) How does NexTel Xstream TV connection work?

The NexTel Xstream TV connection runs on Android 9.0 Pie-based Android TV OS and through the Google Play Store has access to 5000+ Apps and Games. Popular streaming OTT apps can be installed along with the usual DTH functionality.

3) What is NexTel Xstream TV Set-Top Box?

The NexTel Xstream TV set top box is a DTH Set Top Box (STB) which is integrated with a Chromecast so that it can stream OTT content in addition to the usual DTH services.

4) Does NexTel Xstream TV connection need the Internet?

The NexTel Xstream TV requires a WiFi connection or a mobile hotspot to connect to the Internet. The video streaming quality of NexTel Xstream TV also depends on the Internet speed.

5) Why should I buy an NexTel Xstream TV connection?

NexTel Xstream TV is one of the best and the most feature-rich and technologically advanced DTH connections in India. It provides the customers access to the OTT content and comes with a built-in Chromecast. The Android TV OS also helps you install apps and games directly on your TV, making it a Smart TV.

6) What is covered in the price that I pay for a new NexTel Xstream connection?

Included in the cost is the NexTel Xstream TV set top box and the outdoor unit (dish antenna, wire) installation fees, the remote and delivery fees. We also give you subscription to linear TV channels in the same cost – the validity of which is as per the offer available for you. With every NexTel Xstream TV connection, you also get free 3 months subscription to the content on the NexTel Xstream Play app – which is pre-integrated with the NexTel Xstream TV connections and free 3 months subscription to Amazon Prime.

7) Can I continue watching OTT/online content on the NexTel Xstream TV if I do not subscribe to linear DTH channels/packs?

In order to avail the features of the NexTel Xstream TV including accessing OTT apps and online content, you have to keep your NexTel DTH account recharged with a minimal monthly amount. We have monthly packs beginning as low as Rs 153.

SIM-Related FAQs
=========================

 SIM Activation & KYC

Q1. How do I activate a new SIM card?
Insert the SIM into your phone and restart it. You’ll receive a welcome SMS. If not, follow the activation steps provided or call the activation number.

Q2. How long does SIM activation take?
Usually within 4 hours, but it can take up to 24 hours in some cases.

Q3. What documents are required for SIM activation?
Valid ID proof (like Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport) and address proof. KYC is mandatory as per government rules.

Q4. How do I check if my SIM is activated?
Try making a call or checking the network bar. You can also call customer care or check status via the telecom provider's app/website.

Call & SMS Issues

Q5. Why am I not receiving OTP messages?
Check:
- Network signal strength
- If your SMS inbox is full
- DND (Do Not Disturb) settings
- If the correct number is registered with the service
Try restarting the phone or re-inserting the SIM.

Q6. Why can’t I make or receive calls?
Make sure:
- The SIM is active and inserted properly
- The plan/recharge is valid
- Airplane mode is off
- No call barring or forwarding is set

Internet Issues (4G/5G)

Q7. Why is my mobile internet very slow?
Slow internet may be due to:
- Network congestion in your area
- Weak signal strength
- Background app usage
- Incorrect APN settings
You may be asked for your location or PIN code to check area-specific issues.

Q8. How can I improve my mobile data speed?
Try:
- Switching between 4G/5G manually in phone settings
- Restarting your phone
- Checking APN settings
- Moving to an open area with better signal

SIM Not Working

Q9. Why is my SIM showing 'No Service' or 'Invalid SIM'?
This could be due to:
- SIM card damage
- Expired or suspended number
- Improper insertion
Try inserting the SIM into another phone to check if it works there.

Q10. What should I do if my SIM card is not working at all?
Visit the nearest service center or request a SIM replacement. KYC will be required again.

 Website/Payment/Login Issues

Q11. What should I do if the telecom website/app is down?
Wait a few minutes and try again. You can also:
- Clear your browser cache
- Use a different device or browser
- Try the mobile app if the website is down

Q12. My recharge/payment failed but money was deducted. What now?
Wait for up to 2 hours for automatic reversal. If it doesn't happen, contact support with:
- Payment reference number
- Time and amount of the transaction

Q13. I can’t log into my telecom app or website. What can I do?
Make sure:
- Your mobile data or Wi-Fi is working
- Your number is entered correctly
- OTP is received
If login still fails, use the “Forgot Password” or contact customer care.
